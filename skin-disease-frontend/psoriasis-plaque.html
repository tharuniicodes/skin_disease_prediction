Psoriasis (Plaque)

Plaque psoriasis is the most common type of psoriasis, appearing as thick, raised patches with scales. It can occur on elbows, knees, scalp, or back.

Treatment options include moisturizers, topical medications, light therapy, and lifestyle adjustments. A dermatologist can help choose the best plan.
