Melanoma

Melanoma is a serious form of skin cancer that develops in pigment-producing cells. It can appear as a new mole or a change in an existing mole, often showing irregular borders, multiple colors, or rapid growth.

Early detection is very important. If you notice any suspicious mole changes, it is recommended to seek medical advice immediately. Prevention includes using sunscreen, limiting sun exposure, and regular skin check-ups.
