Eczema

Eczema is a common skin condition that causes dry, itchy, and inflamed skin. It can appear as red patches and may flare up due to allergens, stress, weather changes, or certain skincare products.

Managing eczema usually involves regular moisturizing, using mild soaps, avoiding triggers, and sometimes applying medicated creams. If symptoms are severe or persistent, consulting a dermatologist is recommended.
